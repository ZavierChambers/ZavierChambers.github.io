About

I’m Zavier Chambers. Online, most people know me as TheeComputerGuy. Same person on this site as on stream: curious, direct, and more interested in understanding systems than chasing hype.

Professionally, I live at the intersection of security, systems, and business analysis. I help turn fuzzy needs into clear requirements, then turn those requirements into work that actually ships. Most “technical problems” start as communication problems.

I don’t worship tools. I care about outcomes and understanding. I push back on speed-over-quality thinking, overproduction, and performative complexity. This site exists as a living library of things meant to be trusted, used, and understood.

If something here helps you think more clearly or build something better, then it’s doing its job.
