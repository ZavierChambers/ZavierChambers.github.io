Structured Requirements Capture

Capture requirements clearly and consistently. This page helps you think — not overthink.

Project Context — Why does this exist?

Business Requirements — Why the organization cares

These describe goals and outcomes, not system behavior.

Stakeholder Requirements — Who wants what

Capture expectations from specific roles or groups.

Functional Requirements — What the system must do

Describe system behavior. Avoid technical implementation details.

Non-Functional Requirements — How well it must behave

Consider performance, security, availability, auditability, and usability.

Acceptance Criteria — How we know it works

Testable and observable. Given / When / Then works well.

Built for clarity. Not bureaucracy.
